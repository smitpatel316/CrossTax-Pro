# User Research: Cross-Border Tax Filing System

## Research Date
February 20, 2026

## Project Context
Build a CPA-level cross-border tax filing system for US-Canada individuals.

---

## User Personas

### 1. US Citizen in Canada (Expat)
- **Profile:** American who moved to Canada for work
- **Needs:** File US (1040) + Canadian (T1) returns
- **Pain Points:** 
  - FATCA reporting confusion
  - Foreign Tax Credit calculations
  - TFSA/RRSP implications
  - Determining tax residence

### 2. Canadian Citizen in US
- **Profile:** Canadian working in US under TN visa or other
- **Needs:** File US return + Canadian departure tax
- **Pain Points:**
  - US citizenship-based taxation
  - Canada pension plan contributions
  - Exit tax calculations

### 3. Dual Citizen
- **Profile:** Born with both citizenships
- **Needs:** Optimize tax position in both countries
- **Pain Points:**
  - Claiming treaty benefits
  - FBAR/Form 5471 requirements
  - Which country claims primary residence

### 4. Cross-Border Worker
- **Profile:** Lives in one country, works in another
- **Needs:** Deduct remote work expenses, treaty claims
- **Pain Points:**
  - Days-in-country tracking (substantial presence test)
  - Remote work deduction rules
  - Employer withholding complications

### 5. Snowbird (Seasonal Resident)
- **Profile:** Winters in US, summers in Canada
- **Needs:** Part-year tax returns for both
- **Pain Points:**
  - 183-day rule calculations
  - State/provincial tax treatment
  - Healthcare implications

---

## Key User Questions

1. **"Am I a tax resident of the US, Canada, or both?"**
2. **"How do I avoid being taxed twice on the same income?"**
3. **"What forms do I need to file?"**
4. **"Can I contribute to RRSP/TFSA as a US person?"**
5. **"What happens if I miss a filing deadline?"**
6. **"How do I report my foreign bank accounts?"**
7. **"Can I deduct my home office in the other country?"**
8. **"What about my US Social Security or Canada CPP?"**
9. **"How do I handle cryptocurrency cross-border?"**
10. **"What if I have rental income in the other country?"**

## Regulatory Considerations

### US Requirements
- Form 1040 (individual return)
- FBAR (FinCEN 114) - foreign accounts >$10k
- Form 5471 - foreign corporations
- Form 8865 - foreign partnerships
- Form 8938 - foreign assets

### Canada Requirements
- T1 General (individual return)
- T776 (rental income)
- T1135 (foreign income verification)
- NR6 (non-resident withholding)

### Key Treaties
- US-Canada Tax Treaty (1980)
- Social Security Totalization Agreement
- Treaty Article 7 (business profits)
- Treaty Article 18 (pensions)
